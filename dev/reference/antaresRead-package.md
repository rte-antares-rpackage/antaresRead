# antaresRead: Import, Manipulate and Explore the Results of an 'Antares' Simulation

Import, manipulate and explore results generated by 'Antares', a
powerful open source software developed by RTE (Réseau de Transport
d’Électricité) to simulate and study electric power systems (more
information about 'Antares' here : <https://antares-simulator.org/>).
